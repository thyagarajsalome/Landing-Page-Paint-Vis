Interactive Paint Visualizer

Experience a modern way to preview paint colors in real time.

About the Application

Our Paint Visualizer lets you choose a room and explore over 500 vibrant colors. The app overlays selected hues on base images to offer a realistic preview.

Skills & Technology Used

Developing this interactive Paint application required a unique blend of creative and technical skills. Managing over 500 colors for each room and project – including renaming, background removal, masking, and applying Vray AO and reflections – presented significant challenges. To overcome these, I built a toolbox website that automates these tasks, boosting efficiency by up to 100x. Check out the link below for more details.
