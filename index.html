About Me & My Projects

I am a passionate developer with a focus on creating interactive web applications for the paint industry. My Paint Visualizer project is a cutting-edge tool designed to help customers explore thousands of color variations effortlessly.

With extensive experience in web design and a deep understanding of color theory, I have developed a comprehensive solution that not only showcases color options but also enhances the customer’s decision-making process.

My Paint Visualizer lets you choose a room and explore over 500 vibrant colors. The app overlays selected hues on base images to offer a realistic preview.

Skills & Technology Used

Developing this interactive Paint application required a unique blend of creative and technical skills. Managing over 500 colors for each room and project – including renaming, background removal, masking, and applying Vray AO and reflections – presented significant challenges. To overcome these, I built a toolbox website that automates these tasks, boosting efficiency by up to 100x.

Project Overview

My Paint Visualizer web application uses over 500 unique paint colors, each carefully generated and categorized to provide an immersive experience. Customers can preview color combinations, compare finishes, and visualize how colors transform a space.

Key Features:

  • Interactive color preview and selection
  • Extensive color database with dynamic overlays
  • User-friendly interface with mobile responsiveness
  • Integration with real-world applications to visualize rooms
